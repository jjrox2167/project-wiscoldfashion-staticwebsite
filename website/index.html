Wisconsin Old Fashioneds

A Classic Cocktail with a Wisconsin Twist

History of the Wisconsin Old Fashioned

The Wisconsin Old Fashioned is a beloved cocktail in the Midwest, particularly in Wisconsin. It is known for its unique twist on the classic Old Fashioned, often incorporating brandy instead of whiskey and served with a splash of soda. This variation reflects the local palate and has become a staple in bars across the state.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube or 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange slice
  • Maraschino cherry
  • Club soda (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Muddle the sugar cube and bitters in a glass.
  2. Add the whiskey and stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add ice cubes and garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.
  4. Top with a splash of club soda if desired.
